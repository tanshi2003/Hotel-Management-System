About Rama Krishna Hotel

The Palace Luxury Resort is the largest resort in Bangladesh, and is neatly located at Bahubal, Habiganj, Sylhet, some 171 km away from the hustle-bustle of Dhaka. Immerse in a dreamlike setting of tranquility, where one can indulge in a regal experience like no other. Built over 150 acres of land amid serene green tea gardens, the Resort has 22 exquisite hilltop villas for family or group outings, and 107 spacious guestrooms in the Tower Building. Once inside the majestic resort, The Palace offers a world of exhilarating activities like boating, angling, trekking, and cycling. One can choose to spend a hot summer day at the Water Zone, spend time at the Indoor and Outdoor Game Zones, or try the relaxing experience at the sauna, Jacuzzi, or the Turkish Bath. For movie aficionados, there is the option to experience the amenities of a modern theatre.
